Our Story

Founded in 2020

MaidFinder was born out of a simple need: to make finding reliable domestic help easier and safer for families in the UAE. Our founders, having experienced the challenges of finding trustworthy domestic helpers, decided to create a platform that would bridge this gap with technology, transparency, and trust.

Today, we're proud to be the leading platform connecting families with verified domestic helpers across all seven emirates, with over 1,250+ verified professionals and 3,200+ happy families.
